Freddie Thorne

Freddie Thorne is a known Communist who lived in Birmingham. He married Ada Shelby, and they have one son together, Karl Thorne. He was a Sergeant in the Great War and during their military service, saved Tommy's life and was his best friend when they were young.

Portrayed by actor Iddo Goldberg
