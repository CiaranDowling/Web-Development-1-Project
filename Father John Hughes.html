Father John Hughes

Father John Hughes is a member and representative of The Economic League, working with Patrick Jarvis. It is insinuated that he is a child molester as Thomas Shelby is too disgusted to even shake his hand and has no respect for the man. Also Michael Shelby is a victim of Hughes from when he was first taken in.

Portrayed by actor Paddy Considine
