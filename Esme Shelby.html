Esme Shelby

Esme Martha Shelby (Lee) is a member of the Shelby family as wife to John Shelby. She was formerly a member of the Lee gypsy clan. Their marriage was arranged in order to make peace between the two families and provided a carer for John's four children, from his previous marriage, while also caring for her own 2 children with John.

Portrayed by actress Aimee-Ffion Edwards
