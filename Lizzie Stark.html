Lizzie Stark

Lizzie Stark is a former prostitute living in the city of Birmingham. After Thomas Shelby came back from the War in France, she regularly served him as a prostitute for two years and was known to have given service to many men like him. For a short time, she was engaged to Thomas's brother, John Shelby. She currently works as a secretary and assistant for Thomas Shelby and occasionally does other tasks for the Peaky Blinders.

Portrayed by actress Natasha O'Keefe
