Finn Shelby

Finn Shelby is the youngest of the Shelby siblings and a member of the Peaky Blinders. Unlike his three older brothers, Finn did not fight in the First World War as he was not old enough to be recruited. Though he is a Peaky Blinder, his brothers often watch over his safety as he learns to fit better and grow into the role.

Portrayed by actors Alfie Evans-Meese (Series 1) and Harry Kirton (Series 2-4)
