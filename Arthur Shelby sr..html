Arthur Shelby sr.

Arthur Shelby Sr. is the father of the Shelby siblings. Arthur Sr. walked out on his five children some ten years before the start of the series. The family have rarely seen him since. He is remembered by most of the family as a hustler, a thief, and a whore-monger. However, his eldest son Arthur Jr. still has feelings for him.

Portrayed by actor Tommy Flanagan
