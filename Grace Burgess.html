Grace Burgess

Grace is an Irish protestant from Galway. Her father, a colleague of Chester Campbell in the police force, was killed by the IRA. Grace Helen Burgess was an Irish barmaid who took up employment at The Garrison Pub, and secretly operated as an undercover agent for the Birmingham Police. Her mission involved getting close to Thomas Shelby.

Portrayed by actress Anabelle Wallis
